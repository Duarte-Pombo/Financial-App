> *Your wallet knows how you feel before you do.*

## What is Awared?

Most finance apps tell you **what** you spent. Awared tells you **why**.

Awared is a personal finance tracker built around a simple but powerful idea: our emotions drive our spending in ways we rarely notice. That 11pm snack run. The impulse shopping after a tough day at work. The subscriptions you keep buying hoping they'll fix something they never quite fix.

Awared connects the dots.

By logging your emotional state alongside your transactions — or by letting Awared infer patterns over time — the app builds a living map of your **emotional spending profile**. It surfaces the correlations, gently challenges the habits, and — most importantly — helps you understand the feeling underneath the purchase before nudging you toward healthier responses to it.

---

## The Core Idea

```
Feeling nervous → buy candy → feel better (briefly) → repeat
```

On its own, that candy purchase is just a line item. But Awared sees the pattern:

- **Nervousness** consistently precedes purchases at convenience stores
- Those purchases cluster on Sunday evenings and Monday mornings
- The spend is small individually, but adds up to $80/month

Instead of just flagging the overspend, Awared asks: *what's making you nervous on Sunday nights?* And offers tools — breathing prompts, a journal nudge, a spending cooldown — to interrupt the loop before it starts.

## Philosophy

We believe financial health and emotional health are inseparable. Traditional budgeting tools treat spending as a math problem. We treat it as a human one.

Awared doesn't shame you for your patterns. It helps you see them clearly, understand them compassionately, and — when you're ready — change them intentionally.

> *Spend less on what you don't need. Invest more in understanding why you needed it.*
